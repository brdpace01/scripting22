About

Solid Snake, real name David, also known as Snake, Iroquois Pliskin, and later Old Snake, was a former spy, special operations soldier, and mercenary. He is famed for his military exploits, and thrice averted potential nuclear catastrophe.
Snake’s real name and origins are classified but is a clone from a top secret government project, designed to create the perfect soldier.

Snake also possesses an IQ of 180, is fluent in 6 languages, and knows around, approximately, 21 fighting styles.

Games

Metal Gear Solid 1

MGS1 is a stealth action game. First published in 1998 for the PlayStation 1. The game is a third person action game where the player navigates the environment using Snakes covert and stealth abilities.
After the takeover of an nuclear weapons facility, by a rogue military group, Solid Snake is forced out of retirement to uncover the mystery behind the coup and to put a stop to their leader Liquid Snake.

Metal Gear Solid 2

Metal Gear Solid 2 is the sequel to MGS 1 and was first published in 2001 for the PlayStation 2.
The sequel improved on the gameplay elements of its predecessor, with a new first person aiming mode to target specific points, and expanded move set to evade enemies.
Set two years after the events of the Shadow Moses Incident, Solid Snake receives intelligence of a new type of Metal Gear being transported through the Hudson River. As Snake boards the tanker, it's seized by a group of Russian mercenaries led by Revolver Ocelot, intent on stealing the new Metal Gear.

Metal Gear Solid 4

Metal Gear Solid 4 is the sequel to MGS 1 + 2, and is the final canonical game to feature Solid Snake as the main character. The game was released in 2008 for the PlayStation 3.
Several gameplay elements have been added and improved upon. There is now a over-the-shoulder view, new environments and locations, and an Octo-Camo suit that allows the wearer to blend in with any surface within a few seconds.
Set 5 years after the events of MGS 2, the world is in chaos, the "war economy" is plunging the world into never-ending battles. This fuels the need for private military companies, the five largest of which (together rivaling the power of the entire United States Army) are owned by a single mother company, Outer Heaven, with Liquid Ocelot at the helm. Liquid Ocelot, a fusion of the consciousness of Liquid Snake and the body of Revolver Ocelot, is once again set on world domination. Solid Snake, now also referred to as Old Snake due to his advanced accelerated aging, is dispatched to assassinate Liquid Ocelot.

Teammates

Roy Campbell

Roy Campbell was a former U.S. Army colonel and close friend of Solid Snake. During the events of MGS1, he was taken out of retirement and forced Snake out of retirement to stop the rebellion of their former military unit, led by Liquid Snake.

Meryl Silverburgh

Meryl is the niece of Roy Campbell and the former lover of Solid Snake. She is first introduced in MGS1, and re-appears as the leader of her own team in Metal Gear Solid 4.

Hal "Otacon" Emmerich

Hal Emmerich was the chief engineer of Metal Gear REX. He later became a close friends with Solid Snake after he rescues him during the events of MGS1. He and Snake founded the anti-Metal Gear NGO PHILANTHROPY. The nickname Otacon comes from his fascination with the Japanese Sub-Culture known as 'Otaku.'

Cyborg Ninja

The Cyborg Ninja is a cybernetic ninja who appears in MGS1. He is linked to Snakes past and is known for his obsession with battle. His motives and identity are a mystery.

Enemies

Liquid Snake

Liquid Snake is the main antagonist of MGS. He is a clone of Big Boss, created alongside Solid Snake. Like his brother, Liquid is a highly advanced soldier due to his genetic engineering. As a result, he possesses higher endurance, strength, speed, durability, and recovery rate, the latter of which is demonstrated by him surviving numerous events that would be lethally fatal to others

Big Boss

Although not appearing in the first game, Big Boss is the main protagonist of the prequel games: MGS 3, Portable Ops, Peace Walker, and MGS V. The last of which explains his descent into villainy, and subsequent pc games, Metal gear 1 and 2. During the events of MGS, his deceased body is on the list of demands issued by the rogue takeover of Liquid. Who plans to use his DNA in developing his own nation of super soldiers.

Revolver Ocelot

Revolver Ocelot is well known for being a master gunslinger. He handles his preferred handgun, the Colt Single Action Army revolver, with deadly precision. He is skilled enough to hit his target while ricocheting his bullets off walls and other objects. Although being under command of Liquid his true allegiance is unclear due to him being a double agent.

Psycho Mantis

Psycho Mantis is an extremely powerful psychic and psychokinetic. He is able to control and manipulate other people’s minds, and also has the ability to generate hallucinations. He is also capable of moving objects and use them as deadly projectiles. His gas mask is worn to help prevent the intrusive thoughts of others to enter his own.
